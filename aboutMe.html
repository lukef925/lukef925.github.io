About Me

I moved from Vermont to Utah in 2015 to pursue an Undergraduate degree in communications at the University of Utah. Being an avid skier and mountain biker, I searched high and low for schools that allowed me to be minutes away from the mountains. Upon finding the University of Utah I wasted no time getting out here. Throughout my time in Utah I have held various jobs in ski and bike shops that have given me the freedom to pursue my passions while earning a living. Recently I decided to pick up web development and have submerged myself in the culture. I started with simple HTML and CSS web pages and am currently learning JavaScript as well as various frameworks and tools such as NodeJS. I have a few small projects under my belt but as I progress my knowledge and understanding of software engineering I will update this page so you can keep up with my latest projects.
